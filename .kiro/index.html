How to Play

Objective

Reveal all cells without clicking on any mines!

Controls

  • Left-click: Reveal a cell
  • Right-click: Place/remove a flag on a cell

Rules

  • Numbers show how many mines are adjacent to that cell
  • Use the numbers to deduce where mines are located
  • Flag cells you suspect contain mines
  • Reveal all non-mine cells to win!

AI Hint System

  • Click "Ask AI for Hint" to get suggestions for safe cells
  • The AI analyzes the board and suggests safe moves
  • Each hint includes reasoning and confidence level
  • Suggested cells are highlighted on the board
